Sarojini Market Online is a pandemic baby. We started off as an Instagram page in August 2020, a little after the nation-wide lockdown was just lifted. The country was then coming in terms to living with a pandemic. That was the time when we decided to take Delhi’s favourite Sarojini Market online as an effort to help the vendors with the sales. And of course, to make this insanely popular flea market available for the entire country.
